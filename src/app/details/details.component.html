Improve Communications

Organizing a large-scale event requires sharing detailed location information with a wide range of audiences. Attendees need to know where to check in, vendors need to know where to unload and police & fire need to know exactly where their details are located.

Reduce Event Stress

The problem with sharing all of this information is that traditional methods like event signage and volunteer “ambassadors” can be inefficient and often don’t work as planned. That’s where we come in. Our event site maps have robust geolocation features that provide instant directions for all event participants so everyone can easily find where they need to be, eliminating the stress of getting lost or being in the wrong place at the wrong time. With our app, managing events has never been easier.
